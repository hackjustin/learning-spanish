# Irregular Verb Practice	

Conjugate 'ir' in the present tense for 'yo'.	Yo voy (I go)
	
Conjugate 'ser' in the imperfect tense for 'tú'.	Tú eras (You were)
	
Conjugate 'estar' in the present progressive for 'ellos'.	Ellos están estando (They are being)
	
Conjugate 'hacer' in the future simple for 'nosotros'.	Nosotros haremos (We will do/make)
	
Translate: 'I will be happy tomorrow.'	Seré feliz mañana.
	
Conjugate 'tener' in the preterite tense for 'yo'.	Yo tuve (I had)
	
Translate: 'They used to go to the same school.'	Ellos iban a la misma escuela.
	
Conjugate 'decir' in the future simple for 'ella'.	Ella dirá (She will say)

Translate: 'We are being very careful.'	Estamos siendo muy cuidadosos.
